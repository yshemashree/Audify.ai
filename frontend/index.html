Process

How it works

01

Elaborate

Your input is expanded by Qwen 72B. "Cat" becomes "soft indoor meow, close-mic, slight reverb, domestic shorthair."

02

Search

The description is compared against real audio clips. High similarity score means instant match returned.

03

Generate

No match found? ElevenLabs AI generates a brand new audio file from your description.

04

Deliver

Your audio plays instantly in the browser. Download it as a WAV file for use anywhere.
